There are no available templates in your Local Gallery.

To add templates to your Local Gallery, please follow the steps below:

  • 1. Click the Template Gallery link from within the WorkEngine Administration section of Site Settings.
  • 2. Add new templates to the List.
  • 3. If your Site Collection is not set up to use Live Templates (check your setting here), you need to save all templates by browsing to the template site and clicking the Save Template link.

Once templates have been added to your Local Gallery, they will show up on this screen.
